The Wedding

The ceremony will take place in The Treehouse followed by reception in the gardnes.
Date
Friday Augst 16 2024
Venue info
The Alnwick Garden
Address
The Alnwick Garden, Denwick Lane, Alnwick NE66 1FJ
Timings
The main parts of the day are:
  • Guests arrival: 12.30pm
  • Ceremony: 1pm
  • Reception: from 4pm till late
  • The end: midnight
Attire
Smart casual (think garden party)
Be warned (particularly those from the southern hemisphere): while it is August, there is still a strong possibility it will be grey and cold so please plan accordingly (and hope for sunshine and warm weather!)
Other info
The Treehouse and main gardnes are separated by a short walk (less than 5 mins). The Treehouse will be closed to the public for the duration of our ceremony until around 4pm but the gardens will remain open to the public until around 6pm. Both spaces are well equipped for any wet weather.
The venue is a short walk from the main town. Please let us know if you think you will need a taxi as it is best to pre-book.
